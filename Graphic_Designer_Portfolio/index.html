The Psychology of Color in Branding

Colors evoke emotions and influence purchasing decisions. Red creates urgency, blue builds trust, and green represents nature and health. Understanding color psychology helps create effective brand identities that resonate with your target audience.

  • Red: Energy, passion, urgency
  • Blue: Trust, security, stability
  • Green: Nature, health, wealth
  • Yellow: Optimism, clarity, warmth

Print vs Digital Design: Key Differences

Understanding the fundamental differences between designing for print and digital media to ensure your projects look their best in any format.

Print vs Digital Design: Key Differences

Print design uses CMYK color mode and requires higher resolution (300dpi), while digital design uses RGB and works at 72dpi. Print requires bleed areas and considers paper stock, while digital focuses on responsive layouts and interactivity.

  • Print: CMYK, 300dpi, bleed areas
  • Digital: RGB, 72dpi, responsive
  • Print: Physical constraints
  • Digital: Interactive elements
