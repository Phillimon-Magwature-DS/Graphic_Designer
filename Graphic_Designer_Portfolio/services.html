Frequently Asked Questions

The timeline varies depending on the scope and complexity of the project. Simple projects like a logo design typically take 3 days, while comprehensive branding projects can take 1 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation.

You'll receive all the necessary file formats for your specific project. For logos, this typically includes vector files (AI, EPS, PDF), high-resolution PNGs with transparent backgrounds, and JPEGs. For print projects, you'll receive print-ready PDFs with proper bleeds and crop marks.

While I don't handle printing directly, I work with several trusted printing partners and can recommend the best options for your project. I'll provide print-ready files and can assist with selecting paper stocks, finishes, and other specifications.

Minor changes can often be accommodated quickly after project completion. For more substantial changes or new design needs, we can discuss a new project or ongoing design retainer. All final files are yours to keep and can be modified by another designer if needed.

Most projects require 100% payment upon completion. For larger projects, a 50% deposit to begin work with the remaining 50% due upon completion. I accept various payment methods including Ecocash, PayPal, and bank transfers.
